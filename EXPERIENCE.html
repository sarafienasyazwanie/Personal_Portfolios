Here, I am going to tell you about it.


As you can see the pictures above. They are my friends that I got when I passed to join Office Management Student's Association (OMSA)
In OMSA, I started as normal members for the first semester. After it, my club's advisor choose me to be Leader of Entrepreneurship Exco.
At first, I'm quite scared if I can't do a good job. But as the time goes by, I learnt a lot from my senior and I doing better
in handling work matters relating to entrepreneurship. When I'm in OMSA club, I managed many events such as :

  • Dean List's Camp ( Secretary in this event )
  • "Karnival Usahanita Siri 1 dan Siri 2"
  • “Program Tautan Kasih” with Office Management Club, UiTM Melaka
  • Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) Program at Kampung Jerlun, Kedah

    • I participate in OMSA from zero til I know everything in handling event and dealing with high-rank people.


In January 2017, while waiting for my SPM's result, I worked as part-time teacher at Tadika Shimah, Kangar, Perlis.
At there, I learnt on how to manage babies and toddlers. I also became to be more patiently with people. People may think that handling
toddlers are easy but in reality it's really tough job and drained my energy fastly. But, working with children has reminded me
of how amazing and exciting the world can be. I love seeing things from their eyes, so pure and such a cutie. It makes me a happier and more positive person
in my day-to-day life. Children also encourage me to use my imagination and find new creative ways to do things.
